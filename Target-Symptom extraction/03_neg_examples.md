[NEGATIVE GUIDELINES]
The following should NOT be extracted as technical problems:

1. Invention objectives/goals
   Example: "It is an object of the present invention to provide a motor with improved efficiency."
   → Describes desired outcome, not existing limitation. Do NOT extract.

2. Benefits of proposed invention
   Example: "The proposed resistor reduces heat generation by 40%."
   → Describes advantage of NEW technology. Do NOT extract.

3. Abstract recognition/determination issues
   Example: "The conventional ECU cannot determine driver drowsiness accurately."
   → Inability to recognize/detect/determine. Do NOT extract.

4. Non-technical statements
   Example: "The market demand for electric vehicles is increasing."
   → Background context without technical limitation. Do NOT extract.

5. Symptom names containing Target names (Separation Rule Violation)
   Example: "Battery capacity degradation" when Target is "Battery"
   → Must separate as: Target="Battery", Symptom="Capacity degradation"
