[DEFINITION: Technical Problem]
A "Technical Problem" is the problem that an invention solves - specifically, a negative effect that the current technological state-of-the-art cannot fully address.

[DEFINITION: Problem Target]
A "Problem Target" is the specific physical component, system, or device experiencing the technical problem (WHERE the problem occurs).

[DEFINITION: Symptom]
A "Symptom" is the observable negative effect, limitation, or undesired behavior affecting the Problem Target (WHAT goes wrong).
